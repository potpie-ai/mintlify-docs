Potpie allows you to perform **Spec Driven Development**, on large messy codebases. Our proprietary <a href="/concepts/knowledge-graph" className="mode-link">knowledge graph</a> allows our agents to understand the users context in much great detail required to perform complex **multi-hop reasoning** required for debugging, refactoring, etc
